Pizza Recipe

Description

Couples love to make this homemade pizza recipe together! One person makes the dough while the other preps the toppings. It's also great for entertaining because guests can customize their final meals.

It's a quick and easy recipe for whenever the pizza need strikes once again! Below you will find all the relevant information regarding Ingredients and Steps. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil Leaves
  • Red peppers
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250Â°C.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce on the dough
  3. Top with fresh mozzarella cheese and tomatoes
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until the crust is browned
  5. Remove from the oven
  6. Top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and serve
